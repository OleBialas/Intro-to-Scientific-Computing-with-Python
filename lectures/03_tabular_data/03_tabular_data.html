Analyzing Tabular Data with Pandas

The Problem with Excel

  • Bad at handling large data sets
  • Manual operations are error-prone and not reproducible
  • Automatic conversions threaten data integrity

Why use Pandas?

  • Intuitive and accessible design, resembling spreadsheets
  • Mature ecosystem for statistical analysis and visualization
  • Supports a wide range of file formats (including Excel)
  • Optimized for performance (uses Numpy under the hood)
